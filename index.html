Discover Boise

Get away from it all, away from the sounds of distant highways, pollution, and the daily traffic grind. As the capital and most populous city in Idaho, Boise is the trailhead to lush forrests, sparkling lakes and rivers, and views of soaring mountain peaks.

Nestled in the Treasure Valley in the Southwest corner of Idaho, Boise is a sportman’s paradise. So go ahead and grab your tent, hiking boots, binoculars, bike, fishing pole, or skis... and come find your happy place.
